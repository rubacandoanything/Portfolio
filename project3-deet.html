Customer Managment Dashboard

Business Productivity Tool

This comprehensive dashboard serves as the central operational system for client and financial management. It allows users to efficiently add, organize, and maintain client profiles, storing all necessary contact and relationship data in one place. The platform features robust tools for generating and sending invoices, along with a dedicated system to track client payments and monitor invoice statuses (e.g., Paid, Overdue, Outstanding). By consolidating client data and billing processes, this dashboard streamlines administrative workflows, ensuring accurate financial record-keeping and better management of accounts receivable.
