GreenEase Hiking App

Project: App Design

Greenease Hiker transforms your outdoor adventures into rewarding experiences. Explore curated trails, tap NFC checkpoints along the way, and earn badges as you complete each segment. Track your progress, discover new routes, and celebrate your achievements with every hike. Whether you’re a casual walker or a seasoned explorer, Greenease Hiker makes every journey meaningful.
