AI Powered E-BOOK Product Page

Project: EdTech, AI-Integrated Learning Solutions

This AI-powered platform helps job seekers bridge the gap between their current skill set and the requirements for their desired roles. By uploading their CVs, users receive personalized recommendations for e-books that cover the specific skills they need to acquire. The platform analyzes the user's CV, identifies missing skills for a particular job role, and provides targeted e-book suggestions to help them upskill. The service is designed to assist users in becoming more competitive in the job market by offering tailored resources that directly match their career goals.

Release Date 2024.04
Services Provided UI/UX Design in Figma · User Flow Mapping & System Architecture · Interactive Prototyping with Animations · AI Workflow Planning (CV parsing & skill matching logic) · Visual Design for Landing Page, Skill Match Section, Testimonials & E-book Showcase
