Gaming Community App

Project: App Design

Fungames is the unified social platform built for the modern gaming community, blending the engagement of a lifestyle network with the utility of a creator hub. It allows users to share status updates, photos, and life moments with friends, while simultaneously offering powerful tools to collaborate with other developers and artists on new projects. Beyond creation, Fungames hosts a dual library, allowing users to upload their own finished games and instantly play curated titles directly within the application. It is the single destination for gamers to connect, create, and discover.
